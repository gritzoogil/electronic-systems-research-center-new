Who We Are

What is ESRC built for?

Electronic Systems Research Center provides technical support for research and development related to the field of electronics. We develop prototypes for various fields of research involving electronic systems, offer space and technical mentoring to researchers conducting development work, and engage in the innovation of existing projects by collaborating with stakeholders. Watch how we push the boundaries in semiconductor and nanoelectronics research, empowering students and researchers at Batangas State University.
