What We Study

Research Areas

Our multidisciplinary teams push the boundaries of electronic systems across seven core domains, tackling real-world challenges with cutting-edge methods.

Embedded Systems

Designing efficient microcontroller-based systems for real-time applications in industrial and consumer domains.

Internet of Things (IoT)

Building connected sensor networks and edge computing platforms for smart infrastructure and environmental monitoring.

Artificial Intelligence

Applying machine learning and deep learning to automate inspection, classify signals, and optimize system performance.

Robotics

Developing autonomous robotic systems and control algorithms for agriculture, manufacturing, and assistive technology.

Signal Processing

Analyzing and transforming digital signals for communications, biomedical imaging, and audio/RF applications.

Wireless Communications

Designing and evaluating next-generation wireless protocols, antenna systems, and spectrum management techniques.

Renewable Energy Systems

Advancing solar, wind, and hybrid energy conversion systems with efficient power electronics and smart grid integration.
